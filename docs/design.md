### Binds
 - `esc`:     Quit
 - `W`:       Move Forward
 - `A`:       Move Left
 - `S`:       Move Back
 - `D`:       Move Right
 - `space`:   Move Up
 - `shift`:   Move Down
 - `Q`:       Spin Left about Forward
 - `E`:       Spin Right about Forward
 - `m x`:     Spin Left/Right about Up
 - `m y`:     Spin Up/Down
